Bengal Tiger

The Bengal tiger, also called the royal Bengal tiger (Panthera tigris tigris), is the most numerous tiger subspecies.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Average Length: 7.9 - 10ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: Mangrove forests

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family and are renowned for their power and strength. Tigers are powerful nocturnal hunters that travel many miles to find buffalo, deer, and other large mammals. A hungry tiger can eat as much as 60 pounds (27 kilograms) in one night, though they usually eat less.
